Gym Enthusiast

Junior Developer & Designer

Gym goer. - From Monday to Friday I've set up myself a gym routine: I get off bed at 04:00

I like to be consistent with my schedule everyday. I get up this early to shower and grab a quick breakfast before my gym session. I hop on the first bus into town at 05:30 just so that I can be in the gym for 06:00. - I am currently on a nutrition programme that helps me to stay 'fit' & healthy.

Why do I start my day this early? - Well, after I'm finished with my gym session, I'm back home around 08:00 and I have the rest of the day to do whatever I need to. For instance: go to work, do some studies, plan my next holiday trip, etc.

Other Hobbies:

Travelling

Like most people in the world, travelling is one of my passions. I like to make new friends, learn new languages (or at least learn how to say 'Hello' to people in every country I visit).

I have currently visited 24 cities between Latin America and Europe. - Fluently, I speak 2 languages: Spanish (native language) & English. Although, I can communicate in French and Portuguese, I am not fluent.

Funny Stories

A taxi driver was talking to me in a language that I assumed it was Spanish (due to its similarities to my native language) and I was replying back in Spanish. We kept talking for about 10min until the gentleman told me he was from a city I hadn't heard about before, so I asked in English: sorry, where is that? - He looked at me through the rearview mirror and asked: where are you from? I'm from El Salvador, I said. He then said to me: 'oh, FF sake'. I am from Italy and I was talking to you in Italian because I thought you were Italian and I understood everything you said. - The conversation was in Spanish and Italian and we did not realise. Oh, quite an experience!

I booked a flight ticket to Geneva because it was cheap, but my final destination was Lucerne. Had I known the train ticket was going to cost me more than the flight ticket, I would have booked a different flight. - Little did I know the airport was in French territory, not Swiss.

GIG & Music Festivals

Other than fitness and travelling, music is another of my passions. I've gotten the opportunity to see different artists and bands, like: Guns and Roses, Aerosmith, Metallica, even Shakira and her Waka-Waka song.

I always try to get the front row seat. If I'm going to pay to see a performer, I have to be at the front. - I'm always early in the queue to get in the venue. I am usually the 4th or the 5th one queueing.

I usually go by myself to gigs. I rarely go with friends and when I do, I always lose them because I'm always trying find the best spot closer to the stage.

I am in one of Metallica's concert video with their cover of an Irish song: Whiskey in the Jar. I'm in the minute 3:21 with my Salvadoran flag.
