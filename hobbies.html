Gym Enthusiast

Junior Developer & Designer

Gym goer. - From Monday to Friday I've set up myself a gym routine: I get off bed at 04:00

I like to be consistent with my schedule everyday. I get up this early to shower and grab a quick breakfast before my gym session. I hop on the first bus into town at 05:30 just so that I can be in the gym for 06:00. - I am currently on a nutrition programme that helps me to stay 'fit'.

Why do I start my day this early? - After I'm finished with my gym session, I'm back home around 08:00 which means I have the rest of the day to do anything else. For instance, go to work or do some studies, etc.

Funny Stories

I got lost at London Victoria (train station). I was walking around the station for about 20min trying to find the exit and it was right in front of me the moment I got off the train from the airport.

A taxi driver was talking to me in a language that I assumed it was Spanish (due to its similarities to my native language) and I was replying back in Spanish. We kept talking for about 30min until the gentleman told me he was from a city I did not know about, so I asked in English: sorry, where is that? - He looked at me through the rearview mirror and asked: where are you from? I'm from El Salvador, I said. He then said to me: 'oh, FF sake'. I am from Italy and I was talking to you in Italian because I thought you were Italian and I understood everything you said. - The conversation was in Spanish and Italian and we did not realise. Oh, quite an experience!

I booked a flight ticket to Geneva because it was cheap, but my final destination was Lucerne. I did not know that the train ticket was going to cost me more than the flight ticket. - Little did I know that I was in French territory, not in Switzerland.
